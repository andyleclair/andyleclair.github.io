## LSLB

"Your favorite moped gang's favorite moped gang"

I founded [LSLB](https://www.mopedarmy.com/branches/lslb/) along with some very good friends in ~2012 after the collapse of Boston club The Uglies

I've had the distinct pleasure of serving the club as Top Gun™ which is our version of a Captain, but just for organizational purposes. We handle all club matters as a direct democracy. It mostly works!

We've hosted many rallies (and continue to!) and we probably will host more in the future. Maybe.

## DEATH STAR 

Along with my friend [Jason Bergman](https://www.jbergs.com/) we formed DEATH STAR during our tenure at Northeastern.

We taught ourselves how to properly beatmatch and mix and started throwing parties as an excuse to practice crowd work. Eventually we landed a residency at Boston bar The Savant Project (RIP).

In our tenure we had a few residencies around town, including at Good Life (RIP) and Middle East ZuZu and we played probably every club in town from Middlesex to Rise. 

We got to open for some cool big-name acts including Machinedrum and Drake.
