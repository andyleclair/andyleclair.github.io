The years start coming and they well and truly just do not stop coming.

Been a minute since I updated the ol' blog, where have I been?

Well, I've got two boys: 3 and 1. I've been at home. Trying my best to sleep.

It's been busy times at my day job, too, and something needs to take a back seat, and that's meant blogging. That's fine! This ball is [most assuredly plastic](https://kidlitsurvivalguide.substack.com/p/glass-balls-vs-plastic-balls).

That doesn't mean I haven't been working on any projects, though.

I've been busy working on a ray tracer following [Ray Tracing In One Weekend](https://raytracing.github.io/books/RayTracingInOneWeekend.html). Maybe I could've completed it in a weekend before kids, but these days I'm happy to complete anything that's not strictly necessary.

I'm still waiting for the final render (which, of course, I need to write the blog post) so there will be a separate post about that + learning Coalton (and CL), but if you're curious, the code is available on my Tangled: [Ray](https://tangled.org/andyleclair.dev/ray).

Eventually I'd like to move all my code (and this site too!) over to Tangled, but they don't allow custom domains yet, so that'll have to wait.
